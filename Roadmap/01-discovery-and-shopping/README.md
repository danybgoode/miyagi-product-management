# 01 · Discovery & Shopping

**For buyers.** How people find what they want, evaluate it, and come back to it.

This is the top of the funnel: a fast, trustworthy browsing experience that works on the web and as an installable mobile app, with an AI assistant for shoppers who'd rather just ask.

## Current features
- ✅ **Search & browse** listings with category filters and sensible sorting
- ✅ **Product pages** with photos, price, condition, location, and the seller's shop
- ✅ **Listing variety** — products, services, rentals, digital goods, and subscriptions
- ✅ **Favorites** — save items and find them again
- ✅ **AI shopping assistant** — ask for what you want in natural language
- ✅ **Mobile app feel (PWA)** — installable, with a bottom tab bar and splash screens
