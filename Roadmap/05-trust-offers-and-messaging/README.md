# 05 · Trust, Offers & Messaging

**For buyers and sellers.** The human layer — haggling, talking, and feeling safe enough to transact with a stranger.

P2P commerce lives or dies on trust. This domain covers negotiation, real-time conversation, trust signals, and graceful handling when something goes wrong.

## Current features
- ✅ **Make an offer** — price negotiation between buyer and seller
- ✅ **Messaging** — buyer–seller chat, real-time with push notifications
- ✅ **Buyer trust signals** — reputation/identity context before a deal
- ✅ **Refunds with assisted handoff**
- ✅ **Order visibility** — both buyer and seller can see and act on an order's state

- 📋 Dispute resolution flow (ties into escrow / Compra Protegida)
