# Miyagi Sánchez — Product Roadmap & Feature Poster

> **Mission:** make the best of modern e-commerce — trust, fair payments, negotiation, AI-native shopping — **free and high-quality for everyone in Mexico.**
> Miyagi Sánchez is a multi-seller marketplace where anyone can open a shop, list anything (products, services, rentals, digital goods), and sell with no commission — across the marketplace, their own domain, an embeddable widget, or to AI shopping agents.

This folder is the **product source of truth**. It speaks in plain e-commerce language for product, design, and business — **no engineering or tech specs here** (those live in `tasks/` and the team memory).

## How this roadmap is organized

```
Roadmap/
├── README.md                ← you are here · the product poster (all features)
├── WAYS-OF-WORKING.md       ← how we plan, build, ship (scrum cadence) + tooling
└── <Macro-section>/         ← a product domain (a journey, not a component)
    ├── README.md            ← what this area is, for whom, current features
    └── <Epic>/              ← a meaningful body of work

        ├── sprint-N.md      ← the sprint's user stories (As a… I want… so that…)
```

**Levels:** `Roadmap → Macro-section → Epic → Sprint → User Story`. Each user story is a small, independently shippable slice of value.

## Feature map

### 01 · Discovery & Shopping
- ✅ **Purposeful two-state homepage** (Dirección B «catálogo limpio») — signed-out leads with a value-prop ribbon → curated "Selección de la semana" (featured + price-loudest grid) → "Categorías con vida" (only categories with listings) → a live Vecindario strip → a terminal CTA; **signed-in** swaps in a "Retoma donde te quedaste" favorites rail, an actionable pending-offer alert (≤2), and a seller snapshot. - ✅ Search & browse listings with category filters
- ✅ Filter by listing type (products · services · rentals · digital · subscriptions), with a type badge on every card
- ✅ Mobile filter as an apply-gated bottom-sheet with a live "Ver X resultados" count
- ✅ Rich product pages — **"decide, then act"**: reordered by buyer intent (identify → trust → cost/delivery → act), one dominant primary CTA, a "Pago protegido" cue beside the price, and a fixed bar that never covers content; a **scannable per-category specs table**; **per-type decision blocks** (autos REPUVE + specs · inmuebles icon specs + zone map · services schedule · rentals dates+deposit · digital instant-delivery · subscriptions tiers/toggle · events "Comprar boleto" · unclaimed honest notice); seller trust sits above payment/fulfillment on mobile; an **interactive image gallery** (swipe + dots on mobile, thumbnails + arrows + ←/→ on desktop) with **tap-to-zoom fullscreen lightbox**; a scheme-less seller scheduling link always resolves to the real calendar (storefront + agents), and a personalized event keeps its "Comprar boleto" CTA. Whole layout behind the `pdp_redesign` kill-switch
- ✅ Favorites / saved items
- ✅ **Neighborhood feed** (`/vecindario`) — a read-only "pulse" of the local community: moderator-opted-in community contributions grouped by colonia/zona, a "Tendencias" trending-listings strip, and a "Comercios que destacan" merchant spotlight, all over signals already collected (views · favorites · recency). Read-only and agent-readable (`GET /api/ucp/neighborhood-pulse` + MCP `get_neighborhood_pulse`). Deliberate web opt-in (`web_visible`) — fills as moderators surface items

- ✅ Installable mobile app experience (PWA) — an iOS-26 **Liquid-Glass** bottom bar reading **`Inicio · Mensajes · ⊕ Vender · Favoritos · Perfil`** (icons-only) plus a detached glass **search** bubble, that **hides contextually** (on scroll-down, when the keyboard opens, and on immersive routes like the PDP/checkout/chat). Tapping search raises an in-app glass **bottom-sheet** (keyboard-focused, recent + suggested → Medusa `/l?q=`) with **no page transition**; the header search is demoted in the installed PWA (the sheet is primary there) but **stays on mobile web**. ### 02 · Checkout & Payments
- ✅ Cart and guided checkout
- ✅ Pay by card (Stripe), MercadoPago, SPEI bank transfer, DiMo, or cash
- ✅ WhatsApp / arranged-payment path for in-person deals
- ✅ Manual-payment lifecycle: "payment pending" → buyer marks paid → seller confirms — **durable & hardened**: the buyer's "ya pagué" persists (survives reload) and is one shared state across buyer, seller, inbox + agents; a seller **cannot ship before confirming payment** (UI + server-gated on both ship routes); refund language is honest for SPEI/cash ("registrado — transferencia pendiente")
- ✅ Single-item and multi-seller bundle checkout — the pay-button total always equals the summary (coupon/shipping included); manual methods previewed before placement; stalled online payments show a recovery state, never a false success
- ✅ Two-sided off-platform refund state machine (SPEI/cash): "registrado → transferencia pendiente → confirmado" — the seller marks the transfer sent and **only the buyer's "Recibí el reembolso" closes it**; one shared state across buyer, seller + agents (card/escrow auto-confirm)
- 📋 Buyer protection / escrow ("Compra Protegida") (#3c)

- ✅ Seller subscriptions / recurring offerings
- ✅ Seller coupon codes (create/manage + checkout redemption + usage stats)

- ✅ Unclaimed shops are contact-only & delete actually deletes (seller bug sweep) — an unclaimed (gem) listing's PDP suppresses Buy/Offer/Cart/Bundle and shows direct-contact + "Reclama esta tienda" instead, with the offer/cart/checkout server-gated on one shared `isShopClaimed()` (no more silent-fail offers); and deleting a listing is a **native Medusa soft-delete** — gone from the manage grid, catalog, and edit, with order history intact (no more "Borrador" / edit-404 mismatch). Plus legible accent buttons + a mobile-responsive manage sub-nav
