# Miyagi Sánchez — Product Roadmap & Feature Poster

> **Mission:** make the best of modern e-commerce — trust, fair payments, negotiation, AI-native shopping — **free and high-quality for everyone in Mexico.**
> Miyagi Sánchez is a multi-seller marketplace where anyone can open a shop, list anything (products, services, rentals, digital goods), and sell with no commission — across the marketplace, their own domain, an embeddable widget, or to AI shopping agents.

This folder is the **product source of truth**. It speaks in plain e-commerce language for product, design, and business — **no engineering or tech specs here** (those live in `tasks/` and the team memory).

### 02 · Checkout & Payments
- ✅ Cart and guided checkout
- ✅ Pay by card (Stripe), MercadoPago, SPEI bank transfer, DiMo, or cash
- ✅ WhatsApp / arranged-payment path for in-person deals
- ✅ Manual-payment lifecycle: "payment pending" → buyer marks paid → seller confirms — **durable & hardened**: the buyer's "ya pagué" persists (survives reload) and is one shared state across buyer, seller, inbox + agents; a seller **cannot ship before confirming payment** (UI + server-gated on both ship routes); refund language is honest for SPEI/cash ("registrado — transferencia pendiente")
- ✅ Single-item and multi-seller bundle checkout — the pay-button total always equals the summary (coupon/shipping included); manual methods previewed before placement; stalled online payments show a recovery state, never a false success
- ✅ Two-sided off-platform refund state machine (SPEI/cash): "registrado → transferencia pendiente → confirmado" — the seller marks the transfer sent and **only the buyer's "Recibí el reembolso" closes it**; one shared state across buyer, seller + agents (card/escrow auto-confirm)
- ✅ **Rental line-item pricing** — a rental listing books and pays in one step: the buyer picks dates on the PDP, the exact total (**noches × tarifa + depósito**) is server-recomputed and charged at checkout (every payment method, including manual SPEI/cash), never a single-unit price ignoring the date range. The order (buyer + seller pages, both emails, the chat ledger) shows the dates + itemized deposit; AI agents quote and charge the identical number via UCP `checkout-session`/MCP `get_checkout_options` (`check_in`/`check_out` → `rental_quote`). Behind `checkout.rental_pricing_enabled` — **ON in production** (code default stays OFF as fail-safe) *(flag-ON money smoke owed to Daniel — needs a disposable test rental listing first, none in prod)*
- 📋 Buyer protection / escrow ("Compra Protegida") (#3c)
