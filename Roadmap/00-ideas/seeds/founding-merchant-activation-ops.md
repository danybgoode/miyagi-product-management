# Seed — Founding merchant activation operations

Turn the shipped promoter close workspace into the operating CRM for a 13-stage merchant relationship:
scouted → qualified → permission → preview preparation/delivery → activation scheduled → claimed → payments
ready → three products live → shared → first inquiry → first sale → retained at 30 days. Add field capture for
merchant/contact/WhatsApp/Instagram/location/event/category/current channels/fit/consent/notes/objections,
named steward, next action and stage history. Medusa remains commerce truth; Supabase stores relationship and
consent projections; Golden Beans receives lifecycle events and may mirror them to Attio.

Depends on consent-safe previews and Golden Beans' stable subject/event contract. Groom as the next deep ask;
new Supabase schema and cross-repo event contract trigger the advisory cross-panel offer. Do not build generic
sales automation, duplicate orders/products, or make an external CRM canonical.
