# Seed — Merchant Partner portfolio

Give each Merchant Partner a portfolio view of owned merchants, next actions, overdue/stalled accounts,
signed-human follow-up drafts, reassignment SLA, and 30-day retention work. Extend the shipped promoter
attribution/commission rail only after activation behavior is measured; compensation milestones must reward
approved activation and downstream merchant behavior, not raw leads or shop creation. Out: autonomous outbound,
unsolicited messaging, and changing commission money paths without a separate high-risk decision.
